Meowsteroids

Meowsteroids was my first game development project. Created in GameMaker Studio, this asteroids like game sees you fighting off waves of pesky cats. It is available to play here.

Meowsteroids soundtrack

To add a little more variety to the game experience I created three distinct tracks for the background music. The first, inspired by classic videogame chiptune music, was created in FamiStudio. The other two, a lofi remix and a drum and bass remix, were created in ableton.
